Bio

I care deeply about reliable pipelines and respectful data design. My work centers on QoS datasets, smart traffic light integration, and making data explainable by default. I prefer simple, observable ETL over fragile complexity, and dashboards that trade noise for clarity. I’m happiest when data helps teams ship faster with fewer surprises.

Outside the terminal, you’ll find me tuning metrics definitions, reviewing schema changes, or drying out flaky cron jobs. I believe in guardrails, small diffs, and making problems visible early.
